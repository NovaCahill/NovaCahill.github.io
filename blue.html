Brief story
I did rowing during my high school years for my school's team at Wilbraham, Massachusetts.
It was just a club when I initially joined in the year 2013, and our team eventually worked this club into a recognized varsity team a year later.






In 2016 the crew got the first quad scull of our own, the Queen of the Rubicon.
I was promoted into the coxswain of our scull for that spirng and our scull won a sliver medal at its debut regatta held by Narragansett Boat Club, Providence.
